INSTRUCTIONS

Controls:

WASD - Move your ship

SPACE or LEFT MOUSE BUTTON - Hold to charge shot, release to fire

Mouse - Aim where to shoot

K - Skip wave (when available)

Gameplay:

- Survive waves of enemies

- Charge shots for more damage

- Stay within the arena boundaries

- Kill enemies to earn money

- Visit shop between waves to upgrade

- Chain kills to build style meter
